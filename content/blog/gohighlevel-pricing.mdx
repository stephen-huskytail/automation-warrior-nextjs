## The Starter Plan — $97/Month

**What you get:** Full access to GoHighLevel's core feature set for a single account (one business location).

That "full access" includes:
- CRM with unlimited contacts
- Visual pipeline management
- Email marketing campaigns and automations
- SMS marketing and two-way conversations
- Funnel and landing page builder
- Appointment scheduling and calendar booking
- Reputation management (review requests, Google review monitoring)
- Basic course/membership delivery
- Reporting dashboard
- Workflow automation builder

**What you don't get on Starter:**
- Multiple sub-accounts (you're limited to one account — your own business)
- White-label capability
- API access for some advanced integrations

**Who it's right for:**
A solo business owner, local service company, or single-location business that wants to consolidate their marketing tools and automate follow-up. If you're currently paying separately for a CRM, email platform, scheduling tool, and review management — the $97 Starter plan likely replaces $100–$200/month in subscriptions while adding SMS and automation capabilities you probably don't have.

**The math:** If you convert one extra lead per month because of automated follow-up (very conservative for most businesses), the plan pays for itself.

The Unlimited plan is for anyone managing GHL for multiple businesses:

**The agency economics are stark:** At HubSpot Professional pricing (~$800/month per client), an agency with 10 clients pays $8,000/month in platform fees. **Who it's right for:**
Any marketing agency managing clients, any consultant who's setting up GHL for others, and any business owner running two or more separate business entities. If you have even 3 clients using GHL, Unlimited pays for itself immediately.

## Hidden Costs to Know About

The plan prices are the platform fee. There are additional usage-based costs:

**Email sending:**
GoHighLevel uses Mailgun (or your own Mailgun account) for email delivery. Pricing is approximately $0.001–$0.002 per email. For most businesses sending 5,000–10,000 emails/month, that's $5–$20/month on top of your plan fee.

**SMS and calls:**
GHL uses Twilio (or LC Phone, GHL's built-in phone system) for SMS and calls. Pricing is roughly $0.008–$0.015 per SMS segment and $0.013–$0.022 per minute for calls. For a business doing moderate SMS follow-up, budget $20–$50/month extra.

**Phone number rental:**
A dedicated phone number in GHL costs approximately $1.15/month.

**AI conversation bot:**
GHL's AI bot uses tokens — pricing varies but is usage-based and typically a few cents per conversation. Most businesses find this negligible.

**Branded mobile app:**
If you want a white-labeled mobile app on SaaS Pro, that's an additional $497/month on top of the plan fee.

**Realistic total cost estimate:**
- Starter: $97 + $20–$40 usage = ~$120–$140/month
- Unlimited: $297 + $30–$80 usage = ~$330–$380/month
- SaaS Pro: $497 + $30–$80 usage = ~$530–$580/month

## Which Plan Should You Choose?

**Start with Starter if:**
- You're a single business testing GHL for your own marketing
- You want to consolidate your tools before committing to a larger plan
- Budget is a constraint and you can scale up later

**Go with Unlimited if:**
- You have more than one client or business to manage
- You're setting up GHL for any clients (even 2–3 makes this worthwhile)
- You want API access and more advanced automation capabilities

**Choose SaaS Pro if:**
- You're building a recurring revenue model by reselling platform access
- You want full brand control and a custom domain
- You have 5+ clients who would pay for sub-account access
