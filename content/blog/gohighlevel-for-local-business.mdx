Local business customers respond to SMS at dramatically higher rates than email (open rates 90%+ vs 20–25%). GHL gives you a two-way SMS conversation inbox that your team can use from a web browser.

### CRM for Service Businesses

GHL's CRM doesn't require a PhD to operate. Create contacts, track where they are in your sales pipeline, log notes, assign to team members, and automate follow-up based on pipeline stage.

For a local business, the pipeline typically looks like:

Set up automations at each stage: move a contact to "Appointment Booked" and trigger the reminder sequence. Move to "Appointment Completed" and trigger the review request. Move to "Active Client" and trigger an onboarding or welcome sequence.

**Action:** Send follow-up email with the same ask
