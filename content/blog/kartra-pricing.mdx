Kartra pricing now starts at $59 per month, but the cheapest plan is not automatically the best value. The real decision comes down to contact limits, transaction fees, automation needs, and whether you want Kartra to replace several tools or handle only the basics. With four paid tiers plus a free trial, the 2026 pricing structure gives small creators a lower entry point while reserving the strongest automation and support features for growing businesses.

I reviewed the current published plan details and broke down the costs that matter after the headline price. Here is how to choose without paying for capacity you will not use—or selecting a plan that takes a percentage of every sale.

> **Quick Summary**
>
> - **Essentials:** $59/month or $52/month billed annually; 500 contacts and a 5% Kartra transaction fee
> - **Starter:** $119/month or $99/month billed annually; 2,500 contacts and 0% Kartra transaction fees
> - **Growth:** $229/month or $189/month billed annually; 12,500 contacts and advanced automation tools
> - **Professional:** $549/month or $429/month billed annually; 25,000 contacts and expanded analytics, domains, and helpdesk tools
> - Kartra also offers a 14-day free trial that converts to Starter if you continue
> - For a business making regular sales, Starter is often a better value than Essentials because it removes Kartra's 5% transaction fee

## Kartra Pricing Plans at a Glance

Kartra's monthly and annual options differ enough that annual billing can materially reduce your total cost. The table below uses Kartra's published 2026 pricing.

| Plan | Monthly billing | Annual billing, monthly equivalent | Contact limit | Kartra transaction fee |
|------|-----------------|------------------------------------|---------------|------------------------|
| Essentials | $59/month | $52/month | 500 | 5% |
| Starter | $119/month | $99/month | 2,500 | 0% |
| Growth | $229/month | $189/month | 12,500 | 0% |
| Professional | $549/month | $429/month | 25,000 | 0% |

Annual billing saves up to 22%, depending on the plan. It makes sense once you have validated Kartra in your operation, but I would not lock into a year solely for the discount. Use the trial, build one real funnel, connect your payment processor, and test the email workflow first.

Kartra's 14-day trial gives you a practical way to do that. The current offer provides trial access and then moves to the $119-per-month Starter plan if you remain subscribed.

## Essentials — $59 per Month

Essentials is Kartra's lowest-cost plan. It is built for freelancers, solopreneurs, and creators launching a simple offer.

Published limits include:

- 500 contacts
- 10,000 emails per month
- One custom domain
- Five pages
- One product
- One membership or course
- 30 Kartra AI uses
- Forms, checkouts, funnel mapping, and core analytics
- A 5% Kartra transaction fee on sales

The price looks attractive until sales volume increases. Kartra's 5% transaction fee is separate from the payment-processing fees charged by Stripe, PayPal, or another processor. If you sell $2,000 in a month, that Kartra fee alone is $100—more than the $60 difference between Essentials and Starter.

That makes Essentials best for validating an offer with low or inconsistent revenue. It is less compelling for an established course, membership, or coaching program.

**Choose Essentials if:** you have fewer than 500 contacts, one core product, and want the lowest-cost way to launch before revenue becomes predictable.

## Starter — $119 per Month

Starter is the practical entry point for most businesses that intend to sell consistently through Kartra. It removes the platform transaction fee and expands the system enough to support a real product business.

Starter includes:

- 2,500 contacts
- Unlimited email and SMS under Kartra's published plan language
- 0% Kartra transaction fees
- One custom domain
- Unlimited pages
- Unlimited products
- Unlimited memberships and courses
- Video hosting
- Calendars
- Five team members
- 100 Kartra AI uses

The biggest upgrade is not just capacity. It is the move from one product and five pages to an environment where you can build multiple funnels, products, and course experiences without running into the Essentials ceiling.

Starter also changes the fee math. At $1,200 in monthly Kartra-processed sales, a 5% fee equals $60—the exact monthly price gap between Essentials and Starter. Once sales consistently exceed that point, Starter can cost less in practice while giving you substantially more room to operate.

**Choose Starter if:** you are selling courses, coaching, memberships, or digital products and expect at least modest recurring sales. For many creators, this is the best overall value.

## Growth — $229 per Month

Growth is where Kartra's deeper scaling tools become available. It is designed for businesses that have moved beyond a single funnel and need automation, affiliates, webinars, or more operational capacity.

Growth provides:

- 12,500 contacts
- Unlimited email and SMS under the published plan
- 0% Kartra transaction fees
- Three custom domains
- Funnel simulation
- Webinars for up to 1,000 guests
- Behavioral automations
- Affiliate management
- Surveys and quizzes
- Ten team members
- Helpdesk support

The contact jump is significant: Growth supports five times as many contacts as Starter. More importantly, it unlocks tools that affect how you sell and support customers. Affiliate management can replace a separate partner platform, while helpdesk support and advanced automations reduce the number of integrations required to run the customer lifecycle.

This is also the tier where Kartra begins to function as an operating system rather than a collection of page, email, and checkout tools. If your team is manually tagging leads, moving buyers between sequences, or reconciling affiliate sales in spreadsheets, Growth can eliminate expensive administrative work.

**Choose Growth if:** your list is approaching 2,500 contacts, you need affiliate management or webinars, or automation depth matters more than keeping the monthly subscription low.

## Professional — $549 per Month

Professional is Kartra's high-capacity tier. It includes all Growth features and raises the limits for larger teams and multi-brand operations.

Kartra highlights:

- 25,000 contacts
- Five custom domains
- Real-time funnel analytics
- Helpdesk live chat
- All Growth features

The monthly jump from Growth is substantial, so Professional should solve a specific capacity or operational problem. The 25,000-contact allowance matters for a mature list, and five domains can support multiple brands or business units. Real-time funnel analytics and live chat are valuable when a team is actively optimizing conversion rates and supporting meaningful customer volume.

Do not upgrade merely because Professional is the top plan. If 12,500 contacts, three domains, and standard helpdesk support cover your operation, Growth is likely the more efficient choice.

**Choose Professional if:** you manage a large audience, several brands, or a support-heavy business and can tie the additional cost to revenue, team efficiency, or customer retention.

## The Hidden Cost: Kartra's 5% Transaction Fee

The most important line in Kartra pricing is easy to miss: Essentials charges 5% on sales, while Starter and higher plans charge 0% from Kartra.

That creates a simple break-even point. The difference between Essentials and Starter is $60 per month on monthly billing. At a 5% transaction fee, $1,200 in sales produces $60 in Kartra fees. Above that level, Essentials can cost more than Starter before you consider Starter's higher limits and unlimited products.

Payment processors still charge their normal fees on every tier. Kartra's 0% means Kartra is not adding its own percentage; it does not make Stripe or PayPal free.

Also budget for costs outside the subscription:

- Payment-processing fees
- Optional third-party integrations
- Custom design or migration work
- Additional sending or communication costs if fair-use limits apply
- The labor required to build and maintain funnels

The subscription is only one part of total ownership. The strongest reason to buy Kartra is consolidation: pages, email, checkout, courses, calendars, video, and automation in one system. If you still need several outside platforms, reassess the savings before committing.

## Is Annual Kartra Pricing Worth It?

Annual billing lowers the monthly equivalent to $52 for Essentials, $99 for Starter, $189 for Growth, and $429 for Professional. The annual savings are meaningful, especially on the higher tiers.

My recommendation is straightforward:

1. Start with the free trial.
2. Continue monthly long enough to prove your funnel, email deliverability, checkout, and course experience.
3. Switch to annual billing only after Kartra has become a stable part of your revenue system.

Saving money on the wrong platform is still expensive. Migration time, broken automations, and retraining a team can erase the annual discount quickly.

## Which Kartra Plan Is Best for You?

Use this decision framework:

- **Pre-revenue or testing one offer:** Essentials
- **Selling consistently with fewer than 2,500 contacts:** Starter
- **Scaling funnels, webinars, affiliates, or a larger list:** Growth
- **Managing multiple brands or more than 12,500 contacts:** Professional

For most established creators and coaches, Starter is the sensible beginning. Growth is the strongest long-term value when the automation and affiliate tools replace separate subscriptions. Essentials works as a launchpad, but the transaction fee means you should monitor revenue and upgrade promptly when the math changes.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the cheapest Kartra plan?

Essentials is the cheapest paid plan at $59 per month or $52 per month when billed annually. It includes 500 contacts, 10,000 monthly emails, one product, one course or membership, and a 5% Kartra transaction fee.

### Does Kartra have a free plan?

Kartra does not advertise a permanent free plan. It offers a 14-day free trial that currently transitions to the Starter plan if you continue after the trial.

### Does Kartra charge transaction fees?

Kartra charges a 5% platform transaction fee on Essentials. Starter, Growth, and Professional list 0% Kartra transaction fees. Your payment processor will still charge its standard processing fees.

### Can I change Kartra plans later?

Yes. Starting with the plan that fits your current contact count and feature needs is usually better than overbuying capacity. Review your revenue, list growth, domains, and automation requirements before each billing renewal.

### Is Kartra Starter or Growth better?

Starter is better for businesses with up to 2,500 contacts that need pages, products, courses, calendars, and video hosting without a Kartra transaction fee. Growth is better when you need 12,500 contacts, affiliate management, webinars, surveys, deeper automation, more domains, or helpdesk support.

## Final Verdict

Kartra pricing is competitive when the platform replaces several separate tools. Essentials creates a low-cost entry point, but the 5% transaction fee makes Starter the smarter choice once monthly sales reach roughly $1,200. Growth is the best fit for established businesses using automation, affiliates, or webinars, while Professional should be reserved for organizations that will use its higher contact, domain, analytics, and support capacity.
